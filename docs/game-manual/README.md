## Game Overview

**REBUILT™** is a game where two alliances:
- Score **FUEL** (foam balls) into **HUBS**
- Climb **TOWERS** at different levels
- Navigate obstacles (BUMPS and TRENCHES)
- Compete in autonomous and teleoperated periods

**Match Structure:**
- **Autonomous**: 20 seconds
- **Teleoperated**: 2 minutes 20 seconds
- **End Game**: Final 30 seconds (all hubs active)

## Key Game Elements

- **HUB**: Scoring target (47in × 47in) with hexagonal opening
- **TOWER**: Climbing structure with 3 levels (LOW, MID, HIGH rungs)
- **FUEL**: 5.91in diameter foam balls (scoring elements)
- **BUMPS & TRENCHES**: Obstacles on the field

## Scoring Highlights

- **FUEL in active HUB**: 1 point (AUTO or TELEOP)
- **TOWER Level 1**: 15 points (AUTO), 10 points (TELEOP)
- **TOWER Level 2**: 20 points (TELEOP)
- **TOWER Level 3**: 30 points (TELEOP)
- **Bonus Ranking Points**: ENERGIZED, SUPERCHARGED, TRAVERSAL
